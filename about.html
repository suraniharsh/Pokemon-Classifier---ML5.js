About Us

Welcome to Pokehunt, your ultimate companion for exploring and identifying Pokémon. Built using ML5.js and Teachable Machine by Google, our platform leverages machine learning to classify Pokémon from images.

We've designed Pokehunt to be simple, fast, and user-friendly, making it perfect for Pokémon fans, students, or anyone curious about Pokémon. The website works great on all devices, thanks to modern tools like Tailwind CSS.

Our goal is to make learning about Pokémon interactive and exciting. We hope you enjoy using Pokehunt as much as we loved building it!
